## Severity guide

- **P0** — active security, data-integrity, or production outage risk.
- **P1** — launch-blocking functional, accessibility, performance, or deployment defect.
- **P2** — material quality or operational gap that should be fixed before launch.
- **P3** — low-risk polish or maintainability improvement.
- **BLOCKER** — cannot be safely resolved without a product decision or external owner action.
